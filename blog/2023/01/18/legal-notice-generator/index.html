Impressum and Datenschutzerklärung Generator

Posted on Jan 18, 2023

As someone with previous experience setting up websites, I knew that a website / blog aimed at German / European audiences would require legal notices such as “Impressum” and “Datenschutzerklärung” / “Privacy Policy”.

Before publishing this blog, I began researching whether there were any free generators available online. I found many that required me to answer 1000 questions and provide my email address in order to receive the generated text, but that was not what I wanted or liked.

I then started comparing the legal notices of different German websites and soon realized that many of them used the same text blocks. Being clever, I began searching for these text blocks and came across the website of Anwaltskanzlei Weiß & Partner.

Anwaltskanzlei Weiß & Partner offers free generators for Impressum and Datenschutzerklärung. You don’t have to, but you can provide an email address to receive updates in case the generators are updated. Another plus is that the Datenschutzerklärung generator provides the resulting text in German, English and French.

Thank you Anwaltskanzlei Weiß & Partner
